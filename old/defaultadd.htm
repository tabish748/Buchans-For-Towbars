Welcome...

to Buchans for Towbars, Scotland's leading Towbar specialists.


Established in 1984, we are a family run business with over 25 years experience supplying and fitting quality towbars for the Retail, Trade and DIY markets.

As an NTTA Quality Accredited Centre we are committed to providing the highest standards of quality and service to our customers.

We are the largest independent fitters in Scotland with depots in both Glasgow and Edinburgh, offering a towbar fitting service as well as mail order from all of the industry’s leading manufacturers such as Brink and Witter.

We stock a comprehensive range of essential towbar accessories as well as roof systems and cycle carriers.

Our fully trained and experienced fitters can fit your towbar, electrics and any towbar accessory efficiently and safely at our premises. We guarantee first class installation, aftercare and advice to all our customers.
