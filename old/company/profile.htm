Company Profile

Buchans for Towbars was established by brothers in 1984 as a secondary venture within Buchans Group.

Now the largest independent supplier and fitter of quality towbars to cars, vans, 4x4's and commercial vehicles in Scotland. At Buchans for Towbars we only use the highest quality materials from leading manufacturers.

We stock over 1400 towbars at any one time from A1, Thule, Bosal, PCT, Westfalia and Witter along with numerous towing accessories. We have 11 dedicated, fully trained, experienced fitters who can fit your towbar, electrics and any towbar accessory efficiently and safely at our premises in Glasgow and Edinburgh. We also operate a mail order service for the trade and DIY supply markets.

As an Quality Accredited Centre we are committed to providing the highest standards of quality and service to our customers. First class installation, aftercare and advice is guaranteed and all of our towbars are fitted with a manufacturers lifetime guarantee and comply with current legislation.
