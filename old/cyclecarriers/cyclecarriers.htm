Cycle Carriers

Buchans for Cycle Carriers

At Buchans for Towbars we stock an extensive range of cycle carriers from Maxxraxx, Thule and Witter.

We offer a wide range of towbar mounted cycle carriers to fit the conventional flange ball, or the continental style swan neck ball as well as a range of 4x4 wheel mounted cycle carriers. If you need to carry bikes on the roof of your vehicle we also have a range of fully lockable roof mounted cycle carriers.

Please click on the logos below for full details of the carriers available from the industries leading manufacturers.


Maxxraxx Cycle Carriers
The Maxxraxx cycle carrier will carry up to 4 bikes and adapts for skis and snowboards. A pratical rack that allows you to carry carry and tow with bikes fitted.
Thule Cycle Carriers
If you have a car with a towbar installed, we have several bike carrier solutions to choose among, as well carriers for transporting bikes on the roof of the car.

Witter Cycle Carrier
New Improved Witter towbar mounted cycle carrier system for cars, vans and 4x4's. Lets you carry upto 4 bikes and tow at the same time.
