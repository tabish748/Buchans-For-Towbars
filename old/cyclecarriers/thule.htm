Cycle Carriers | Thule Cycle Carriers

Bike Carriers for towbar mounting
If you have a car with a towbar installed, we have several great Thule bike carrier solutions to choose among.  With our patented towbar attachments you can be sure the carrier and bikes stay securely in place. There are carriers for two to four bikes and with practical features such as tilt functions, bike frame locking and more.

Thule HangOn: 974 (3 Bike), 9708 (4 Bike)
The easiest and most cost effective way to carry up to bikes. (The Basic Carrier)
Ride On: 9502 (2 Bike)/9503(3 Bike)
A bike carrier that is easy to mount and load.
Euroclassic: 908 (2/3 Bike)/ 909 (3/4 Bike) with adaptor 9081
Brilliant and compact top of the range carrier for up to four bikes.


Bike Carriers for roof mounting
To transport a bike on the roof of the car is a truly practical solution. The bike stays securely mounted and gets less dirty there than when transported on the rear door/towbar. Perhaps you do not have a towbar fitted, or want to use it to pull a caravan or trailer instead.

Thule ProRide: 591
An elegant, easily-installed bike carrier with a clever new design.

Thule FreeRide: 530
A safe and reliable bike carrier that fits most bikes.
Thule Outride: 561

For bikes fitted with front forks and removable front wheel.
