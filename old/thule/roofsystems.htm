Roof Systems

Buchans for Roof Systems

At Buchans for Towbars we stock Thule, the world's largest supplier of car roof systems.

The Thule complete roof loading system from Sweden carries almost anything you can imagine. The range includes load carriers, roof boxes and accessories for transporting bikes, skis, snowboards, canoes, surfboards and more.

Thule offers the following solutions, allowing your vehicle to expand to match your needs. Simply follow the links below for full details:

Load Carriers
Whether you want to discover new worlds by ski, bike, kayak or canoe, the trip begins with the load carrier. A load carrier from Thule is the basis for the entire transportation system that enables your car to grow with the task at hand, whatever your car may be.

Bike Carriers
No matter what car you have or how you want to transport your bike, Thule has a solution to do the job. And do it easily, safely and stylishly, we might add. Every single bike carrier is tested to endure external stress, year after year, without losing any of its functions.

Roof Boxes
Roof boxes are ingenious solutions for many transportation problems. Not only are they easy to load and unload, but you will also avoid cramming your baggage into the car while still transporting it safely. In Thule's assortment, you will find everything from spacious and exclusive to tough, sturdy, short and convenient boxes.

Ski Carriers
Whether you want to transport skis or snowboards Thule has an extensive range of carriers to choose from. From the exclusive and feature-packed - designed to look really good on your car - to the basic versions that do their job without fuss.

Water Sport Carriers
Our wide assortment can help you make transporting of kayaks, canoes, small boats, sailboards or surfboards a piece of cake. Of course you need to put in some muscular effort to get the gear onto the roof of your car, but we have done our best to spare your back.

Carrier Baskets
If you need to transport long or bulky items, these carrier baskets will make it easy to secure your load. You may choose between the sturdy, black steel baskets or the more elegant aluminium ones.

Special Carriers
These load carrier accessories are designed to meet the needs of the demanding craftsmen and all others who need to carry timber, ladders, drain pipes and similar types of bulky items.


At Buchans for Towbars, any Thule product purchased from us we provide a full installation / advice service for your roof system
